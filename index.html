Manjaly Builders – Best Building Contractors in Kerala

Founded by Mr. Jaison J Manjaly in 1994, Manjaly Builders is a trusted name in South India’s construction industry. Headquartered in Thrissur, Kerala, we’ve grown over 30 years with a skilled team of 125 professionals, delivering durable and innovative building solutions.
